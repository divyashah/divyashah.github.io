Postdoctoral Reseracher in Robot Design

Italian Institute of Technology

I am a postdoctoral researcher with the iCub Tech facility at Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), working on the mechatronic design and development of compact and powerful humanoid joint modules for the ergoCub project aiming for human-robot collaborations for risk prevention in warehouse and medical scenarios.

My active involvement with the collegiate robotics club during my undergrad, aspired me to pursue higher education in robotics.
